Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with me

I need your raw footage, any specific requirements or style preferences, your target platform (YouTube, TikTok, etc.), and a brief description of your vision. If you have reference videos or branding guidelines, those are helpful too!

Turnaround time depends on complexity and project scope. Short-form content (TikTok, Reels) typically takes 2-3 hours, while long-form videos usually take around 5 hours. For clients I work with regularly, I can often expedite the process since I'm already familiar with your style and preferences.

I primarily use Adobe Premiere Pro for editing, After Effects for motion graphics and animations, and Photoshop for custom thumbnails and graphics. This ensures professional quality and compatibility with industry standards.

I offer unlimited revisions, but within reason, to make sure the project moves forward efficiently. My goal is to deliver exactly what you envision, and I'm happy to refine the edit until you're completely satisfied. For ongoing partnerships, this collaborative approach becomes even smoother as I learn your preferences.

Absolutely! I'm experienced with all major platforms and their specific requirements. I can optimize your content for YouTube (16:9), TikTok/Instagram Reels (9:16), Instagram feed posts (1:1), or create multiple versions from the same footage.

Yes! I can adapt to any style or branding guidelines you have. Whether it's a specific color palette, font choices, animation style, or overall aesthetic, I'll ensure your videos maintain brand consistency across all content.

Definitely! I provide professional color grading to enhance the visual appeal of your footage, and I create custom motion graphics, text animations, logo animations, and kinetic typography to make your content stand out.

I deliver videos in high-quality MP4 format, optimized for your chosen platform. I can also provide different resolutions (1080p, 4K) and formats if needed. Files are shared via Google Drive or WeTransfer for easy download.

Pricing depends on the project scope and long-term collaboration. Please leave me a message so we can talk it out and build a package that works best for both of us. I value building lasting partnerships with creators and businesses, and I'm happy to create custom solutions that fit your ongoing content needs and budget.
